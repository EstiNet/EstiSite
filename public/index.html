About

EstiNet is a Minecraft server born out of a desire for a server that is both unique and fun, stable and reliable. Built from the ground up, EstiNet uses custom built tech, on a server setup curated and tuned for over 4 years. EstiNet has a passion for quality. Our minigames and gamemodes were hand picked, and each built with love. That's what makes us different, different by block.
